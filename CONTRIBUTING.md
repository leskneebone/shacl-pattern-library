# Contributing

The project is currently an exploratory personal repository. Discussion and proposals are welcome, but acceptance does not imply standardisation or a commitment to maintain an identifier indefinitely.

A pattern proposal should include a completed [pattern contract](docs/pattern-contract.md), rationale, scope and non-goals, Core/SPARQL feature declaration, dependencies, conforming and non-conforming examples, expected reports, engine test evidence, performance and information-disclosure notes, multilingual behaviour, provenance, licence compatibility, and migration consequences.

Changes to released semantics require a new version. Editorial fixes that cannot affect validation may retain a version but must be recorded. Never silently broaden targets or strengthen constraints.

By contributing, you agree that your contribution may be released under CC0 1.0. Do not contribute material you cannot license on those terms.
