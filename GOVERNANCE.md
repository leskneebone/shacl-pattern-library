# Governance

This repository is presently maintained by its owner as an experiment. There is no appointed standards body, institutional sponsor, namespace authority, or publication authority.

The maintainer may accept, revise, or reject proposals, but published experimental material must record authorship and provenance. A future transfer requires a public decision record covering stewardship, trademark/name use, namespace control, release authority, conflict resolution, succession, and archival continuity. Transfer must not rewrite provenance or make old development identifiers appear institutionally endorsed.

Maturity labels express evidence, not voting status or authority. The proposed labels are `experimental`, `candidate`, `stable`, and `deprecated`; their release gates remain to be ratified.
